About Data Wand

We're building the simplest way to get paper forms into your computer.

Data Wand was built by people who watched office workers waste hours retyping the same forms every day. Church offices, property managers, insurance agencies — all doing the same thing: staring at a paper form, then typing it into a computer. Over and over.

We knew there had to be a better way. So we built one.

We're a small team focused on one thing: making paper-to-digital as easy as taking a photo. Everything runs on-device by default. For complex forms, optional AI-assisted recognition is available with built-in guardrails that protect sensitive information — always opt-in, never automatic.
