Turn Paper Forms into Digital Data

Capture handwritten forms with your iPhone, auto-fill web forms instantly. No cloud required - all processing happens on your devices.

Privacy First iPhone + Chrome No Server Needed

How It Works

1

Capture

Point your iPhone at any paper form. The camera captures high-quality images ready for OCR processing.

2

Extract

Apple Vision OCR reads handwriting directly on your iPhone. No cloud uploads - your data stays private.

3

Fill

Auto-populate web forms with extracted data or export to Google Sheets. Save hours of manual data entry.

Why DataWand?

Privacy First

All OCR processing happens on your iPhone using Apple Vision. No data ever leaves your devices or touches any cloud servers.

No Server Needed

Your Chrome extension connects directly to your iPhone over WiFi. Peer-to-peer connection means no middleman.

Smart Detection

Automatically detects form fields, checkboxes, and tables. Create templates once, reuse for hundreds of forms.

Flexible Export

Auto-fill any web form or export directly to Google Sheets. Choose the workflow that fits your needs.
